About

Living in the Heart of the Central District

Junior Apartments is located at 507 22nd Ave in Seattle's vibrant Central District — one of the city's most culturally rich and connected neighborhoods. Enjoy walkable access to local dining, parks, and public transit.

Managed by Guide Management, we're dedicated to providing a comfortable and responsive living experience for every resident.

  • Central District location
  • On-site laundry
  • Responsive maintenance
  • Online rent payments
  • Near public transit
  • Walkable neighborhood
